# p09: Cold-asking for opportunities sometimes produces results that wouldn't otherwise materialize

The author cites personal examples: cold-emailing GiveWell as a junior led to an internship, initiating group houses with near-strangers led to living situations, initiating romantic relationships expanded options.

These are anecdotal (N=1 per example) but directionally consistent. The claim is not that asking always works, but that not-asking guarantees the opportunity doesn't exist.
