# p03: The Gale-Shapley result is simultaneously acceptor-pessimal

Every acceptor receives the worst partner they could have in any stable matching. The same algorithm that maximally benefits proposers maximally disadvantages acceptors.

This is the dual of p02. It means the asymmetry is not incidental — it is structural and absolute.
