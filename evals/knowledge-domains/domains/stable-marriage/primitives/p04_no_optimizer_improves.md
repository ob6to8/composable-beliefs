# p04: No external optimizer can improve any single proposer's outcome

Even with perfect information and unlimited computation, no rearrangement of the stable matching can give any individual proposer a better partner. The algorithm already achieves the theoretical maximum for every proposer simultaneously.

This eliminates the possibility that proposer-optimality is merely "good on average" — it is individually and collectively maximal.
