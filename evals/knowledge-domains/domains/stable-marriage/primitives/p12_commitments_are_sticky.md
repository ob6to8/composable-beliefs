# p12: Commitments are sticky due to transaction costs

Once matched, switching partners/jobs/housing has costs: emotional investment, moving logistics, reputation, social ties. The algorithm allows costless switching (an acceptor drops a current match for a better proposal). Real commitments resist renegotiation.

This cuts both ways — stickiness can trap you in suboptimal matches, but it can also create investment that improves matches beyond their initial quality.
