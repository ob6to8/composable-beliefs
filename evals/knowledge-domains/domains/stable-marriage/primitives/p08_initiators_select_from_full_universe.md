# p08: Initiators select from the full universe of options; passive recipients select only from those who approach them

A person who asks can target anyone. A person who waits can only choose among those who happened to find and approach them. The initiator's choice set is structurally larger.

This is empirically observable and does not depend on the Gale-Shapley mapping. It is true independently of the mathematical result.
