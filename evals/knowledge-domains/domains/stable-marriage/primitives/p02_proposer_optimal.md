# p02: The Gale-Shapley result is proposer-optimal

Every proposer receives the best partner they could have in ANY stable matching. Not on average — in every case. Even a deity optimizing for a single proposer could not improve their outcome beyond what the algorithm produces.

This is the strongest form of optimality: simultaneously optimal for every member of the proposing side.
