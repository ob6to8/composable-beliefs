# c03: Empirical evidence supports the advantage of initiative

Independent of the algorithm, initiators demonstrably access a larger choice set, and anecdotal evidence shows cold-asking producing opportunities that wouldn't otherwise exist.

This compound stands partially independent of the mathematical proof — p08 (larger choice set) is observable without Gale-Shapley. This means even if the bridge (c02) fails, there's a weaker empirical case for initiative.
