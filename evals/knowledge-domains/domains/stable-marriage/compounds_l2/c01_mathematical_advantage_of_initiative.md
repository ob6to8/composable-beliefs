# c01: The mathematical advantage of initiative is absolute and proven

The Gale-Shapley algorithm proves that proposers achieve individually and collectively maximal outcomes, while acceptors achieve individually and collectively minimal outcomes. No rearrangement can improve any proposer's result. The asymmetry is structural, not statistical.

Within the algorithm's assumptions, this is airtight. The question is whether the assumptions hold outside the algorithm.
